Cheesburger Tacos

Combining two favorites - what could go wrong?

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef chop meat
  • 1 full Romaine lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • French dressing
  • Sweet relish
  • White vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Burger buns
  • Onions
  • Pickle slices
  • Shredded Mexican cheese mix (Cheddar cheese will work also)
  • American cheese slices
  • Ma Deuce Steak Seasoning
  • Standard Issue Salt & Pepper
  • Alder Smoked Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Add all these next ingredients into a bowl and mix thoroughly
    • 4 teaspoons sweet relish
    • 1 tablespoon finely minced white onion
    • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
    • 1/8 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup Mayo
    • 2 tablespoons French dressing
  2. When the secret sauce is mixed place in the refrigerator
  3. Dice up the Romaine lettuce into tiny pieces
  4. Dice up your onion into small squares
  5. Place your onions and lettuce to the side until the end
  6. Unpack your chop meat and place in a skillet over medium heat
  7. Season the beef with Ma Deuce Steak Seasoning
  8. Season the beef with Standard Issue Salt & Pepper
  9. While the beef is cooking separate your burger buns
  10. With a rolling pin flatten each burger bun both sides separately
  11. Place the flatten burger tops in a taco rack
  12. Once the buns are in the rack layer 1 slice of American cheese per taco shell
  13. Add a few sliced pickles on top of the American cheese
  14. Keep stirring your beef as it cooks (Salt the chop meat with Alder Wood Smoked Sea Salt to taste)
  15. Sprinkle the shredded cheese on top of the chop meat and stir until itâ€™s completely melted
  16. Once the chop meat is cooked add a spoonful to each taco shell
  17. Sprinkle the onion and lettuce on each taco on top of the meat
  18. Drizzle your secret sauce on top of each taco!
