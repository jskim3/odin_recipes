Instant Pot Chicken Adobo

Chicken adobo is a filipino recipe. It is a delicious savory dish. I don't think the potatoes, onions or garlic is traditional but I think they taste delicious cooked in the gravy.

This recipe is fast and easy to make in the Instant Pot, so it's a great weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • 4 Bone-in Chicken Thighs or Legs
  • 1 Large Russet potato
  • 1 Large white onion
  • 4 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup White Vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Water

Steps

  1. Set Instant Pot to Saute mode and brown chicken to crisp up skin
  2. While chicken is browning, cut potato into ~1 inch cubes
  3. Cut onion into quarters
  4. Lightly smash garlic cloves - just enough to break them open
  5. Combine soy sauce, vinegar, and water
  6. Once chicken skin is crisp, be sure to scrape up any bits stuck to the bottom of the Instant Pot so they don't burn during pressure cook
  7. Add the rest of the ingredients - potatoes, onions, garlic, bay leaf and soy sauce/vinegar mixture
  8. Set to Pressure Cook, More for 8 minutes
  9. Once the pressure cook is complete, manually release the pressure
  10. *OPTIONAL* Open the lid and set to Saute high to reduce the liquid to create a thicker gravy
  11. Serve with or over white rice
